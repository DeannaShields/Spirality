 
List of files and functions for Spirality




PRIMARY FUNCTIONS: These are functions the user might call

- Spirality: Measures pitch angle of a .fits spiral

- GenSpiral: Creates synthetic .fits spiral.
- MultiSympart: Adds together multiple symmetric parts (m=2 + m=3, etc.) of a .fits spiral.
- SpiralArmCount: Counts spiral arms of a .fits spiral. Pitch angle must be input.
- Sympart: Takes the symmetric part (m=2, m=3, etc.) of a .fits spiral.
